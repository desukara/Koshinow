About Foxjin / 狐ジン

The industry taught Foxjin as much as the classroom did.

After years of working with schools across Japan, one pattern became impossible to ignore. When staffing needs changed unexpectedly, schools were often forced to make important decisions under pressure and with fewer options than they deserved. The challenge was rarely keeping lessons running. The challenge was finding the time and flexibility to make the right decision without disrupting students, parents, or daily operations.

Foxjin / 狐ジン was created after seeing that situation repeat itself across the industry. Schools needed time. Students needed consistency. Yet many of the available solutions seemed designed around filling positions rather than supporting schools. Foxjin exists to provide experienced native English teaching support while schools maintain control of their next decision.

Industry Perspective

Experience from every corner of the classroom.

Corporate Programs

Foxjin has worked within large educational organizations where consistency, communication, and professional standards are essential. These environments revealed how quickly staffing decisions can affect schedules, student experiences, and daily operations when multiple moving parts depend on one another.

Independent Schools

Foxjin has also worked alongside independent schools built through years of personal investment, reputation, and trust. These environments provided a firsthand understanding of what school owners face when every staffing decision affects not only the classroom, but the long-term health of the school itself.

The Gap Between

Working across both environments revealed a pattern that appeared far more often than expected. Schools frequently needed flexibility, time, and practical support, yet many of the available solutions seemed designed around filling positions rather than solving the school's actual situation. That gap became the foundation for Foxjin.

What Matters Most

Support that works with your school, not around it.

Every school has its own culture, expectations, and way of doing things. Effective teaching support should strengthen what is already working, not disrupt it. That means adapting quickly, respecting established systems, understanding classroom expectations, and helping students feel confident and comfortable from the moment the lesson begins.

Foxjin was built around a simple principle: support should reduce pressure, not create it. Clear communication, professional preparation, reliability, and respect for the school's existing operation remain at the center of every classroom, lesson, and partnership.

How Foxjin Helps

Practical support when schools need flexibility, time, and stability.

Classroom Continuity

Support for schools facing unexpected absences, staffing changes, schedule adjustments, or other situations where maintaining a stable classroom experience becomes the immediate priority.

Time to Decide

Not every situation calls for an immediate permanent hire. Foxjin provides experienced teaching support while schools evaluate options, interview candidates, and make long-term decisions with confidence.

Additional Teaching Capacity

Support for trial lessons, new classes, seasonal programs, expanding enrollment, and periods when an extra experienced teacher helps reduce pressure on existing staff and operations.

When Schools Reach Out

Because students cannot put their education on hold.

Every school owner knows the feeling. A teacher leaves. A schedule changes. Plans suddenly become uncertain. Yet the students still arrive, the lessons still need to happen, and parents still place their trust in the school every day. The pressure is real, and the responsibility does not disappear simply because the situation became complicated.

That is usually when schools reach out to Foxjin. Not because they need a quick fix, but because they need time to make the right decision without sacrificing the classroom in the meantime. Students deserve consistency. Schools deserve options. Foxjin exists to help protect both.
